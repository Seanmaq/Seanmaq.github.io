Art Experience

Throughout my years in schooling I've acquired experiences with working with multiple mediums of artmaking, such as both acrylic and oil painting, drawing, sculpture, photography and shortfilm making; with painting and drawing mainly being my artmaking expertise.

Experience with multiple artmaking practices allow me as a Visual arts educator to assist students in sparking and further developing their found interests in any specific form of artmaking, whether it be in guiding them through the process of utilising such mediums or in guiding them in how they can further reach their full potential within a desired medium.
