Teaching Experience & Attributes

Some of my Prior Experiences in Teaching Visual Arts

Experience

I've had multiple experiences in teaching, with some that correlate to Visual arts teaching. During the back end of 2023, I had the opportunity to assist Sydney Catholic Schools in a Visual Arts Workshop that assisted then year 12 students with their Visual Arts HSC Major works. This allowed me to have some insight into the range of understandings of what a Visual Art needs in order to fully assist in their students in fully developing and realising the grnadest potential of their body's of work.

As well as this, I also had experience in the teaching within the public primary sector, as I taught scripture to a class of year 5 at Burraneer Public Primary, as part of the (then De La Salle) St Aloysius Catholic College Cronulla catechism outreach program with local public primary schools, in partnership with the local parish of St Aloysius Gonzaga Catholic Church.

In teaching within both the Primary and Secondary sectors, I was able to have a full grasp of the ins and outs of the role of teachers within their classrooms. To further link with my teaching philosophy; The role of an educator is guide students to achieve their fullest potential no matter their ability.

Teaching Placement Experience

So far during my college schooling at ACU (Australian Catholic University) Strathfield, I was able to have the opportunity to go on a Teaching Placement during June of 2024. I was assigned to assist in Visual Arts Teaching at St Mauron's Maronite College Marrickville, a preschool to year 12, where I was teaching roughly 1-2 lessons a day. During my placement I had the opportunity to teach grades 7, 10 and 12. All classes allowed to have a grasp of the different range of learning abilities, in further to fully understand students and how they learn within a Visual Arts learning environemnt.

Throughout my placement, I was also able to be mentored by my supervising teacher, Mr Mark Lunnon, who was able to guide me in ways and methods in improving my approach to pedagogy and to better build a professional rapport with students to keep them engaged with learning the content.
