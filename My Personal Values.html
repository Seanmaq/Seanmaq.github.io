My Personal Values

Personal Values Towards Life and Teaching

My Experience

Growing up in a Catholic family and community there are persoanal experiences and values that may assist in my role as an educator, as well as how they also may assist students.

Though my routes be within the Catholic tradition with my experience in working within my local parish and archdiocese (Sydney), I hope to integrate such values of Dignity, Common Good, Compassion, etc, throughout all aspects of my teaching in relation to relevant topics.

Integration in Teaching

The integration of such Catholic values doesn't mean tat I'll have a whole section explicitly dedicated to such, but means in a cross-curriculum sense, that I will provide slight links that may provide students insight into the meaning of such values and how they can utlise them in everyday life. However, I do intend to implicate such values in the classroom/learning environment, as it best alllows students to examplify such.

Values:

  • Family
  • The Common Good
  • Dignity
  • Compassion
  • Respect Life
  • Integrity
  • Service and more...

Mission

My mission in educating is to pass down the values that I hold dear and communicate it coinciding with what is being taught. I don't aim to convert anyone, as no two people think alike, but I aim to assist in growing these values in students so that they may have the opportunity to utilise them throughout their everyday life.
