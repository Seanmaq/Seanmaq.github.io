Teaching Philosophy

The role of an educator is guide students to achieve their fullest potential no matter their ability

My teaching philosophy revolves around the idea that we - as educators - must always have the goal of fully realising and guiding students in achieving their full potential no matter the situation or level of learning ability. Guidance is a life-long learning process not only for our students but for us educators as well.

In guidance, we can assist students in developing skills and understanding useful in further growing their life skills, such as respect, problem-solving, working cooperatively, etc.
